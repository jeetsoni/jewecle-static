Cancellation Policy

At Jewecle, we understand that circumstances may change, and you may need to cancel your subscription or booking. We strive to make the cancellation process as transparent and convenient as possible. Please review the following cancellation policy:

  1. Subscription Cancellations:
    • - Monthly Subscriptions: Monthly subscriptions can be canceled at any time. Upon cancellation, your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing cycle, and you will not be charged for the subsequent month.
    • - Annual Subscriptions: You can cancel your annual subscription, but refunds will not be provided. Your subscription will remain active until the end of the current subscription period.
  2. How to Cancel:

    To cancel your subscription, please follow these steps:

    - Subscriptions: Log in to your account on the app, navigate to the "Subscription" section, and select the option to cancel.

  3. Switching/Upgrading the Subscription:

    You may switch or upgrade the subscription from monthly to annually or vice versa. But please note that switching the current plan to a new plan will result in the loss of subscription fees. Neither refund for the previous plan will be provided nor deduction in the new plan will be allowed.

  4. How To Switch/Upgrade:

    - Switch or upgrade the subscription: Log in to your account on the app, navigate to the "Subscription" section, and select the option to switch or upgrade.

  5. Refunds:

    - Subscription Refunds: We do not provide refunds for partially used subscription periods.

  6. Contact Us:

    If you have any questions or need assistance with canceling your subscription or booking, please don't hesitate to contact our customer support team at jeweclebusiness@gmail.com

  7. Changes to Policy:

    Jewecle reserves the right to modify or update this cancellation policy at any time. Any changes to the policy will be communicated to users through the app or via email.

By using our app and services, you agree to adhere to the terms mentioned in this cancellation policy.

Thank you for choosing Jewecle. We appreciate your understanding and cooperation.
