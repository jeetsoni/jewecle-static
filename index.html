About Us

At Jewecle Business, we are dedicated to revolutionizing the way of traditional gold jewellery industry operates between manufacturers and retailers. Founded in 2022, our journey began with a vision to improve the brand identity and jewellery product diversification to both side. In added that, we also strive for the new AR technological era in jewellery industry. Since then, we have grown into a dynamic team of passionate individuals driven by innovation and a commitment to excellence.

Our mission is clear: to serve every jeweller through our best quality platform which can help them to grow their business. Through Augmented Reality technique, we strive to eliminate old catalogue and make the order system. Whether it's DESIGHAT designs, JADATAR designs, or ROSEGOLD designs, we aim to organise the whole B2B jewellery market.

At the heart of our company is our unwavering dedication to our customers. We believe in building lasting relationships by providing unparalleled quality and after-sales services to our customers. Our customer-centric approach ensures that every interaction with us is seamless and rewarding.

Innovation is in our DNA. We continuously push the boundaries of the real gold and diamond jewellery market by our innovative website and application-based software. Our team of experts works tirelessly to stay ahead of the curve and anticipate the needs of tomorrow.

As we look to the future, we are excited about the possibilities that lie ahead. With quality photo search and jewellery GPT concept, we are poised to create futuristic jewellery software. Together, we can create a world where the normal jeweller can impact as same as corporates.

We look forward to having your interest in building this excellent futuristic journey.
